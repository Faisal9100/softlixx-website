Effective Blockchain Strategies for Your Business

Dec 1, 2023

Blockchain is a decentralized, digital ledger that records transactions on multiple computers. It allows multiple parties to record transactions without the need for a central authority, making it a secure and transparent way to record and track transactions.

Here's how it works:


1. Transactions are recorded: When a transaction occurs, it is recorded on a "block" along with a unique code called a "hash." This block is then added to the end of the chain, creating a record of the transaction.
2. The transaction is verified: The transaction is then verified by a network of computers, called "nodes." These nodes work together to ensure that the transaction is legitimate and not fraudulent.
3. The transaction is added to the blockchain: Once the transaction has been verified, it is added to the blockchain and becomes a permanent part of the ledger.
4. The transaction is transparent: Because the blockchain is a decentralized and transparent ledger, anyone can view the transaction history and verify its authenticity.
