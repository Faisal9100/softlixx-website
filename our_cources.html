Front End Development

The course will cover topics such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as frameworks such as React and Angular. Students will learn how to build responsive layouts, create animations and effects, and integrate with APIs and back-end systems. Throughout the course, students will have the opportunity to work on practical projects that allow them to apply their learning and develop their skills. These projects may include building websites and applications from scratch, as well as customizing and extending existing ones. These projects may include building websites and applications from scratch

Back End Development

In this course, students will learn the fundamental technologies and techniques used in back-end development, including server-side languages such as Python and Node.js, and databases such as MySQL and MongoDB. They will also learn how to build and deploy server-side applications, and how to design and implement RESTful APIs. Throughout the course, students will work on hands-on projects to apply their knowledge and skills, and will have the opportunity to build and deploy their own back-end applications.

Graphic Designing

The course will cover topics such as design theory, typography, color theory, composition, and layout. Students will learn how to use design software such as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop to create and edit graphics. They will also learn how to effectively communicate their ideas through the design process and present their work to clients or stakeholders. Throughout the course, students will have the opportunity to work on practical projects that allow them to apply their learning and develop their skills. These projects may include designing logos, brochures, posters, and other marketing materials.
